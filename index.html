Lecture Summarizer

This is an AI-powered lecture summarizing app – built to help you save time and focus on what matters.

About the App

Lecture Summarizer is a powerful AI tool that turns long, detailed lectures into quick, easy-to-read summaries. Whether you're a student, educator, or lifelong learner, this tool helps you keep track of key ideas without the hassle of note-taking.

Key Features

✔️ Upload your lecture audio or video
✔️ AI generates concise summaries
✔️ Organized output: bullets, paragraphs, highlights
✔️ Access on phone or laptop

How It Works

1. Upload a recording or transcript of your lecture
2. Our AI analyzes and summarizes the content
3. You receive a clean, structured summary
